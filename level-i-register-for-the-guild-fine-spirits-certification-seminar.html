Level I: Register for the Guild On-Line Spirits Certification Exam

For any of our Level 1 Spirits Seminars you can sign-up for the diploma exam either before or after you attend the seminar. Or, before or after you complete the on-line lecture component of the seminar. If you sign-up for the exam and take it within two weeks of taking the seminar use the following registration:

Register: Level I Spirits Seminar On-Line Exam ($50)  
Take the exam at your convenience.  When you sign up we will send you the information you will need to take the exam. 

If you are signing up for the exam more than 2 weeks after you attended the seminar, use this registration:

Register: Level I Spirits Seminar On-Line Exam ($100) - After 2 weeks  
Take the exam at your convenience.  When you sign up we will send you the information you will need to take the exam. 

When you sign up for the on-line exam the Guild will send you an email with instructions on how to take the exam and a second email, from the company we use to administer the exam, inviting you to their website to the take the exam.

Registration & Cancellation Policy:

You must register for a seminar or course through the International Wine Guild. Online registration closes 1 day prior to class start date. If you need to register after the close date, please contact the International Wine and Spirits Guild at 303-296-3966.

Because we purchase wines and food based on the number of registrants, no-shows are charged a non-refundable fee equal to $525 per person, unless other arrangements are made at least 7 working days in advance of the seminar.

If for any reason a seminar is canceled we will phone or e-mail you 48 hours prior to the start of the seminar. If a seminar is canceled at the last minute due to bad weather (this is very rare) we will reschedule the seminar. However, if a seminar is held and you decide not to attend because you believe the weather is bad then it will be treated as a no-show.

It is highly recommended that you register early, your credit card will not be charged until seven days in advance of the course. We must have a minimum students registered in order to form a course. If you are traveling from out of town to attend, please do not make travel arrangements until two weeks in advance of the seminar or course.
