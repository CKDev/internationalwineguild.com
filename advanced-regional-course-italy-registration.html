Level III: Advanced Regional Course: Italy Registration

Schedule of 10-Week Advanced Regional Course: Italy

This 10-week course meets from 6 PM -10 PM once a week for a total of 40 contact hours. Course fee is $1,995 (includes registration fees, handout materials, 50+ wine tastings, and instruction). Older vintage wines are tasted in this course.

Register: Denver, CO April 12 - June 14, 2021
Mondays, 6PM to 10PM ($1995)

Register: Denver, CO October 11 - December 13, 2021
Mondays, 6PM to 10PM ($1995)

Schedule of 1-Week Intensive Study Advanced Regional Course: Italy

This 1-week intensive study course meets from 8 AM - 5 PM daily, Monday through Friday, for a total of 40 contact hours. Course fee is $2070 (includes registration fees, handouts, 50+ wine tastings, lunch each day, and instruction). Older vintage wines are tasted in this course.  

Register: Denver, CO April 19 - 23, 2021
Daily, 9 AM to 5 PM ($2070)

Register: Denver, CO October 18 - 22, 2021
Daily, 9 AM to 5 PM ($2070)

*There is no difference in course content and instruction between the 10-week and 1-week courses.

There are three intensive study courses offered back-to-back. You may sign up for one, two or all three back-to-back advanced courses that are offered during a month.

If for any reason a class is canceled we will phone or e-mail you 48 hours prior to the start of the class. If a class is canceled at the last minute due to bad weather (this is very rare) we will reschedule the class. However, if a class is held and you decide not to attend because you believe the weather is bad than it will be treated as a no-show. It is highly recommended that you register early, your credit card will not be charged until seven days in advance of the course. We must have a minimum students registered in order to form a course. If you are traveling from out of town to attend, please do not make arrangements until two weeks in advance of the course.
