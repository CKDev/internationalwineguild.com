Level 2: Advanced Wine Course

A Level 2 Course


View Dates, Locations and Register for This Wine Class

 

If you are a professional or serious wine enthusiast this course is for you! It covers the top wine producing countries in the old and new world. If you are currently in the wine or hospitality industries you can start you Guild education with the course.

This course provides the wine regional knowledge and tasting skills to become a wine aficionado.

Course covers:

  • Tastings of 40+ wines from around the world 
  • France
  • Italy
  • German and Austria
  • Spanish and Portugual, including fortified wines
  • North American Wine including Mexico, Canada and the USA
  • Australia and New Zealand 
  • South America: Argentina, Brazil, Chile and Uruguay
  • South Africa
  • Noveau New World: China, India, Japan
  • Event Planning
  • Wine Making
  • How to read a wine label and write it correctly for a wine list
  • Sommelier Service (and practical exam)
  • Descriptive and Comparative Wine Evaluation
  • 700+ pages of educational material designed specifically for this course 

Schedule & Format

This course is available in two formats: (1) 10 weeks meeting once a week for 5 hours or (2) an intensive study course meeting from 8:30am to 5:30pm 5 days (Monday through Friday).  When you go to the schedule you have the option to sign up for either format.

Please sign up early, some materials are sent out in advance to help you prepare for one of the final exams.
