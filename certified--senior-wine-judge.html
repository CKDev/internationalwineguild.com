Certified Wine Judge

Want to learn how to judge wine and/or how to organize and manage a wine competition? This is your opportunity!

Course Overview

  • The complete judging process, including event preparation
  • Event organization and management
  • Scoring systems for judging wine and wine and food pairing events
  • Grape and fruit wine judging
  • Dealing with fatigue and common scoring errors
  • Wine and food pairing judging
  • Understanding amateur vs. professional events

Prerequisites

You must have successfully completed both the Advanced Wine Course and the Advanced Wine and Food Pairing Course to take this course.

Certified Guild Competition Wine Judge

The Guild Competition Wine Judge Program is designed to prepare you to judge:

  • Commercial Wine Competitions (in the USA or elsewhere)
  • Wine and Food Pairing Competitions
  • Guild Senior Program Sommelier Evaluations (so you can teach Level 2 Wine Course)

Prerequisites

You must have completed the Certified Wine Judge Course above.
